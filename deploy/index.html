About

Car enthusiasts young and old can enjoy the automotive industry’s latest creations at the Pennsylvania Convention Center during the annual Philadelphia Auto Show.
The Auto Show features hundreds and hundreds of concept, classic, luxury, and exotic cars on display, with every major vehicle manufacturer represented.
All vehicles are arranged by category, making it easy to navigate the 700,000-square-foot display floor.
